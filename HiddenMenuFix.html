Hidden Menu Fix

Sometimes when opening a menu in Cimatron, it will pop up outside of your display window. the steps below will help you move the menu back into view.



  1. Open the menu that is hidden.
  2. It will open off screen, but you can press (alt + space bar) at the same time. This will open a menu on the hidden window.  
  3. Press "M" on your keyboard. 
  4. Then press one of your arrow keys.
  5. Now the menu is locked to your mouse cursor, and you can drop it anywhere you want.
